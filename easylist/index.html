What EasyList does

Built for the stuff that quietly drains your energy

EasyList is not trying to be a massive enterprise system. It is meant to be the clean, fast layer that gets everyday obligations out of your head and into motion.

Planner

Weekly planner that feels usable

View your next seven days in a way that helps you make decisions, not panic. Perfect for errands, reminders, admin work, and light planning.

Bulk Entry

Separate Add Tasks page

Enter multiple tasks in one pass. Good for brain dumps, Sunday resets, and those moments where you suddenly remember twelve things at once.

Progress

Knocked Out Today

See what you actually finished today so the app feels rewarding, not just demanding.

History

Weekly archive

Completed tasks stay organized by week with exact completion dates, giving you proof that you are in fact handling your life.

Focus

Priority with personality

Use playful priority tiers so the system feels human and memorable instead of sterile.

Style

Classic Mode and Candy Mode

Keep the sharper default look or switch into the lighter, brighter visual vibe later. Same product, different energy.
